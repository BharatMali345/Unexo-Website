Unexo India Platform



Unexo India Platform:


  • UNEXO is a platform created under its parent OBOCOW and has various subsidiary functions provides services and facilitates many different aspects of our social life – creating jobs, gig economy, health services, education, food & beverage in addition to creating many opportunities for its global members.

  • In addition to having a virtual/digital platform, UNEXO will also have a physical office presence in each city and most rural towns to encourage membership and create a community of people helping other people with the assistance of our team or government assistance when required and available. This “phygital” (Physical plus Digital) environment combines the best of both cyber and brick-mortar environments.

  • UNEXO Education and Healthcare become crucial services for the society at large while Café and Entertainment are secondary aspects for the enjoyment and networking of members that require physical contact.

  • This is a venture philanthropy that shares much of its profits to benefit the vulnerable and least advantaged people, giving them a leg up to join the middle class of the populace.
